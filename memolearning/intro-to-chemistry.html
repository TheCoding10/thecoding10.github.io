What is Chemistry

Understand the definition and scope of chemistry as a science.

Matter Science Properties
Chemistry is the science that studies the composition, structure, properties, and behavior of matter at the atomic and molecular level.
Branches: organic, inorganic, physical, analytical, biochemistry
