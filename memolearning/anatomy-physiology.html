Anatomy vs Physiology

Understand the difference between structure and function.

Structure Function Relationship
Anatomy is the study of body structures, while physiology is the study of how those structures function. Structure and function are closely related - the shape of a structure determines its function.
Example: The heart's muscular walls (anatomy) enable it to pump blood (physiology)
