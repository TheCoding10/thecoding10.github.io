Unit 1: Money Basics & Financial Mindset

Develop a healthy relationship with money and understand fundamental financial concepts.

What is Money and Its Functions

Understand money as a medium of exchange, unit of account, and store of value in modern society.

Financial Mindset Development

Cultivate a growth mindset toward money and develop positive beliefs about wealth building and financial success.

Money Psychology and Emotions

Explore the emotional aspects of money decisions and learn to manage financial stress and anxiety effectively.

Financial Values and Priorities

Identify your core financial values and align your spending and saving decisions with your life priorities.

Common Money Myths Debunked

Recognize and overcome limiting beliefs about money, wealth, and financial success that hold people back.

Financial Stress Management

Learn techniques to manage money-related stress and maintain mental health during financial challenges.

Goal-Setting for Financial Success

Set SMART financial goals and create actionable plans to achieve short-term and long-term financial objectives.

Building Financial Confidence

Develop confidence in financial decision-making through education, practice, and gradual skill building.

Unit 2: Income & Earning Potential

Understand different income sources and strategies to maximize earning potential.

Types of Income Sources

Learn about salary, wages, freelance income, business profits, investment returns, and rental income streams.

Active vs Passive Income

Distinguish between income requiring active work and passive income that generates money with minimal ongoing effort.

Salary Negotiation Skills

Master the art of negotiating salary increases, benefits, and compensation packages effectively and confidently.

Side Hustles and Gig Economy

Explore opportunities to earn additional income through freelancing, gig work, and side business ventures.

Career Development Planning

Create strategic career plans to advance professionally and increase earning potential over time.

Skills Investment for Higher Pay

Identify valuable skills to develop and invest in education or training that leads to higher-paying opportunities.

Multiple Income Streams

Build financial security by diversifying income sources and reducing dependence on a single employer.

Income Tax Basics

Understand how income taxes work, tax brackets, and strategies to optimize your tax situation legally.

Unit 3: Budgeting & Money Management

Master essential budgeting skills and money management techniques for financial control.

Creating Your First Budget

Learn step-by-step how to create a personal budget that tracks income, expenses, and savings goals effectively.

50/30/20 Budgeting Rule

Apply the simple budgeting framework: 50% needs, 30% wants, and 20% savings and debt repayment.

Zero-Based Budgeting

Master zero-based budgeting where every dollar is assigned a specific purpose before spending occurs.

Envelope Method

Use physical or digital envelopes to allocate cash for different spending categories and control expenses.

Tracking Expenses Effectively

Implement systems to monitor and categorize expenses to identify spending patterns and optimization opportunities.

Budget Apps and Tools

Explore digital budgeting tools like Mint, YNAB, and spreadsheets to automate and simplify money management.

Managing Irregular Income

Create budgets that work for freelancers, contractors, and others with variable monthly income streams.

Budget Troubleshooting
