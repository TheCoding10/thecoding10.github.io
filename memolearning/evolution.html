What is Evolution

Understand the basic definition and concepts of biological evolution.

Change Time Populations
Evolution is the change in heritable traits of biological populations over successive generations. It results from processes that change allele frequencies in populations.
Key concepts: descent with modification, common ancestry, natural selection

Common Misconceptions

Address frequent misunderstandings about evolutionary theory.

Misconceptions Facts
Evolution is both a fact (organisms have evolved) and a theory (explanation of how evolution works). It doesn't mean "just a guess."
