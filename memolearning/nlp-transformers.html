Definition Scope Applications

‚Ä¢ Semantics: Meaning of words and sentences

Challenges in NLP

Understand the fundamental challenges that make natural language processing complex and difficult.

Core NLP Challenges:
‚Ä¢ Ambiguity: Multiple valid interpretations
‚Ä¢ Context dependency: Meaning changes with context
‚Ä¢ Variability: Different ways to express same idea
‚Ä¢ World knowledge: Implicit assumptions and common sense
‚Ä¢ Cultural and social factors: Language use varies across groups
Data Challenges:
Language data is often noisy, biased, and sparse for many languages and domains. Quality labeled data is expensive to create, and language constantly evolves with new words and meanings.
