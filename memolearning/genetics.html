What is Genetics

Understand the science of heredity and genetic variation.

Heredity Variation Genes
Genetics is the study of genes, genetic variation, and heredity in organisms. It explains how traits are passed from parents to offspring and how genetic variation arises.
Key concepts: genes, alleles, chromosomes, inheritance, mutation
