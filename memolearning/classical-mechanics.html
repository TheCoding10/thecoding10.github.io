What is Classical Mechanics

Understand the fundamental principles and scope of classical mechanics.

Newton Motion Forces
Classical mechanics is the branch of physics that deals with the motion of objects from projectiles to parts of machinery, as well as astronomical objects such as spacecraft, planets, stars, and galaxies.
Classical mechanics applies to objects much larger than atoms and moving much slower than the speed of light.
