What is Matter

Understand the basic definition and properties of matter.

Mass Volume Particles
Matter is anything that has mass and takes up space. It is composed of particles (atoms or molecules) that are in constant motion.
Key characteristics: mass, volume, particle composition, energy content

Four States of Matter

Learn about the primary states of matter and their distinguishing features.

Solid Liquid Gas Plasma
Solid: Fixed shape and volume
Liquid: Fixed volume, variable shape
Gas: Variable shape and volume
