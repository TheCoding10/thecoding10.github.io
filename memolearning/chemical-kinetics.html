What is Chemical Kinetics

Understand the branch of chemistry that studies reaction rates.

Speed Time Mechanism
Chemical kinetics is the study of the rates of chemical reactions, the factors that affect these rates, and the mechanisms by which reactions occur.
Key aspects: reaction speed, controlling factors, molecular pathways, practical applications
