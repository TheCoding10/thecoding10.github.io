Types of Business Organizations

Understand different ways businesses can be structured and organized based on size, industry, and ownership.

Businesses can be classified by size (micro, small, medium, large), industry sector (primary, secondary, tertiary), ownership structure (private, public, non-profit), and geographic scope (local, national, global).
Case Study: Amazon started as a small online bookstore (small business, tertiary sector, private) and evolved into a large multinational corporation (large business, multiple sectors, public company) demonstrating how businesses can transform over time.
