Learn the fundamental structure of interconnected financial markets, institutions, and infrastructure worldwide.

Historical Evolution and Milestones

Trace the development of global finance from the gold standard to modern digital systems.

Major Financial Centers Worldwide

Explore the roles of London, New York, Tokyo, Hong Kong, and other leading financial hubs.

Interconnectedness and Systemic Risk

Understand how global financial linkages create both opportunities and systemic vulnerabilities.

Globalization Impact on Finance

Analyze how economic globalization has transformed financial markets and capital flows.

Financial Market Integration

Study the process of market integration and its effects on pricing, efficiency, and risk.

Cross-Border Capital Flows

Examine patterns and drivers of international investment and capital movements.

Global Financial Stability Mechanisms

Learn about institutions and frameworks designed to maintain global financial stability.

Unit 2: International Monetary System

Explore the framework governing international monetary relations and exchange rates.

Bretton Woods System and Evolution

Understand the historical foundation of modern international monetary arrangements and their evolution.

International Monetary Fund (IMF)

Learn about the IMF's role in global monetary cooperation, surveillance, and crisis assistance.

Special Drawing Rights (SDRs)

Explore the IMF's international reserve asset and its role in the global monetary system.

Exchange Rate Regimes

Compare different exchange rate systems from fixed pegs to floating rates and their implications.

Reserve Currencies and Status

Understand the concept of reserve currencies and the factors that determine international currency status.

Balance of Payments Dynamics

Analyze how countries track international transactions and the implications of BOP imbalances.

Currency Crises and Interventions

Study the causes of currency crises and policy responses including central bank interventions.

Digital Currencies and CBDCs

Examine the emerging role of central bank digital currencies in the international monetary system.

Unit 3: Foreign Exchange Markets

Master the world's largest financial market and currency trading mechanisms.

FX Market Structure and Participants

Understand the decentralized structure of FX markets and the roles of various market participants.

Currency Pairs and Quotations

Learn currency pair conventions, bid-ask spreads, and how exchange rates are quoted globally.

Spot and Forward Markets

Master spot currency transactions and forward contracts for future currency delivery.

Currency Derivatives and Swaps

Explore currency options, futures, and swaps used for hedging and speculation.

Exchange Rate Determination

Analyze the fundamental and technical factors that influence currency valuations.

Purchasing Power Parity

Understand PPP theory and its application in long-term exchange rate forecasting.

Interest Rate Parity

Learn about covered and uncovered interest rate parity and their role in FX markets.

FX Trading Strategies

Explore various currency trading strategies including carry trades, momentum, and mean reversion.
