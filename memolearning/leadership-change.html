Leadership vs Management

Understand the critical distinctions between leadership and management roles and functions.

Vision Setting People Focus Change Agent
Leadership is about setting direction, aligning people, and motivating and inspiring them to achieve a vision. Management is about planning, organizing, controlling, and problem-solving to maintain stability and efficiency in current operations.
