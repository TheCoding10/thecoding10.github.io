Growth vs Development

Distinguish between economic growth and broader development processes.

Key Distinctions:
‚Ä¢ Growth: Quantitative increase in economic output
‚Ä¢ Development: Qualitative improvements in well-being
‚Ä¢ Growth is necessary but not sufficient for development
‚Ä¢ Development includes social and institutional changes
Growth-Development Relationship:
‚Ä¢ High growth may not reduce poverty if inequality increases
‚Ä¢ Quality of growth matters (pro-poor vs pro-rich)
‚Ä¢ Environmental sustainability constrains long-term growth
‚Ä¢ Institutional development enables sustainable growth

Human Development Index

Understand how HDI measures development through health, education, and income dimensions.

HDI Components:
‚Ä¢ Life Expectancy Index: Health and longevity
‚Ä¢ Education Index: Knowledge and learning
‚Ä¢ Income Index: Standard of living
‚Ä¢ Geometric mean of the three indices
HDI Limitations:
‚Ä¢ Doesn't capture inequality within countries
‚Ä¢ Equal weighting may not reflect preferences
‚Ä¢ Missing dimensions (environment, freedom, security)
‚Ä¢ Data quality issues in some countries
# Human Development Index Calculation
def calculate_hdi(life_expectancy, education_index, income_index):
  """Calculate HDI using geometric mean"""
  import math
  
  # Normalize life expectancy (min=20, max=85)
  life_index = (life_expectancy - 20) / (85 - 20)
  
  # HDI is geometric mean of three indices
  hdi = (life_index * education_index * income_index) ** (1/3)
  
  return hdi

def calculate_income_index(gni_per_capita):
  """Calculate income index using log transformation"""
  import math
  
  # Log transformation (min=$100, max=$75,000)
  min_income = math.log(100)
  max_income = math.log(75000)
  actual_income = math.log(gni_per_capita)
  
  income_index = (actual_income - min_income) / (max_income - min_income)
  
  return income_index
