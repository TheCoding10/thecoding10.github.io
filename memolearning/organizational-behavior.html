What is Organizational Behavior

Learn the fundamental definition and scope of organizational behavior as a field of study.

Human Behavior Workplace Performance Scientific Study
Organizational Behavior (OB) is the systematic study of how people behave in organizations and how that behavior affects organizational performance. It combines psychology, sociology, anthropology, and management to understand workplace dynamics.
